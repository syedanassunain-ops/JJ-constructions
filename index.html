FAQ

Frequently Asked.

What is Studio Housify's signature process and timeline?

We follow a structured, hassle-free roadmap: a dedicated bespoke design journey (moodboards, layouts, 3D renderings) followed by our strict 45-day operations process roadmap (covering false ceiling, electrical, civil works, 1-week factory fabrication, 1-week on-site works, painting, and professional cleaning).

Where are Studio Housify's turnkey modules manufactured?

Our modular components—including wardrobes, kitchen modules, and custom furniture—are crafted using state-of-the-art machinery in our advanced factory. This ensures millimeter-level precision, perfect edge banding, and quality standards that traditional on-site carpentry can never achieve.

What warranty and support do you offer on modular interiors?

We offer an industry-leading 10+ years warranty on our modular cabinets and wardrobes, backed by our direct post-handover support, premium branded hardware (Hettich, Blum, and Häfele), and high-grade waterproof marine plywood (Century Ply / Trojan).

Where is the Studio Housify design center located in Bangalore?

Our premium design studio is located in Kalyan Nagar, Bengaluru at 3, Hennur 1st Cross Rd, HBR Layout 4th Block, Kalyan Nagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560043. You are welcome to book a free personal consultation to touch and experience our wide range of premium finishes and material mockups.
